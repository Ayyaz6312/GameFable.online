How to Use the Age Calculator

Our free online Age Calculator helps you determine your exact age from your date of birth. Whether you need to know your age in years, months, and days, or want a detailed breakdown including hours, minutes, and seconds — this tool delivers precise results in under a second.

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Enter your date of birth using the date picker (Day, Month, Year).
  2. Optionally, select a specific date using the "Age at Date" tab to calculate age at any point in time.
  3. Click the "Calculate Age" button.
  4. View your results — including exact age, next birthday countdown, zodiac sign, and day of birth.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Age Calculator work?
Our Age Calculator uses your date of birth and the current date to compute your exact age in years, months, days, hours, minutes, and seconds. It accounts for leap years and varying month lengths for 100% accuracy.
Is the Age Calculator free to use?
Yes, the ToolsHouse Age Calculator is completely free to use with no sign-up, no downloads, and no limits. Use it as many times as you need.
Does the Age Calculator account for leap years?
Absolutely. Our calculator accurately handles leap years (years divisible by 4, with exceptions for century years not divisible by 400) to ensure precise age calculations.
Can I calculate age between any two dates?
Yes, you can use the "Age at Date" option to calculate age or time difference between any two custom dates, not just from birth to today.
How accurate is this Age Calculator?
Our Age Calculator is 100% accurate. It uses precise date arithmetic that accounts for varying month lengths (28, 29, 30, or 31 days) and leap years.
